About Us

Café Bean, part of Coffee Bean Global Limited, is India’s favorite hangout for coffee and conversations. Known as CCD, we source our coffee from thousands of small planters who shape our journey. Our first café opened in 1996 on Bangalore’s Brigade Road, quickly becoming a hotspot for youth. Today, CCD remains a go-to place for meeting, chatting, and enjoying great coffee, growing into the country’s largest organized café chain.
